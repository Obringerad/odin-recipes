Lasagna

Description

Lasagna baked with a sausage meat sauce and parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • Sausage
  • Onion
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Tomato Paste
  • Mozzarella
  • Parmesan
  • Noodles
  • Ricotta

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
