Hamburgers

Description

Juicy, seasoned, succulent, flavorful all beef patties on a delicious semi sweet, soft, fresh bun.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • American Cheese
  • Ketchup
  • Mayo
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Cook patties.
  2. Add cheese.
  3. Arrange ingredients in desired order.
